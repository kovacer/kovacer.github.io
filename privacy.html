Privacy Policy

Effective date: 14 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Future Radar processes information when an authorised user connects a TikTok account or uses Future Radar to prepare and upload content.

1. Information we may process

Depending on the permissions authorised by the user, Future Radar may process:

  • basic TikTok profile information, such as an account identifier, display name and profile image;
  • account-authorisation information, including access and refresh tokens;
  • videos, captions and related information selected by the user for upload;
  • technical information needed to operate, troubleshoot and secure the service.

2. How information is used

Future Radar uses information only to:

  • authenticate an authorised user;
  • connect to the TikTok account selected by that user;
  • prepare and upload content requested by the user;
  • maintain security and diagnose technical problems;
  • comply with applicable legal and platform requirements.

3. TikTok and other third-party services

Future Radar uses TikTok developer services to authenticate users and upload content. Information sent to TikTok is handled according to TikTok's own terms and privacy policy.

Future Radar is an independent service and is not owned, operated or endorsed by TikTok.

4. Data sharing

Future Radar does not sell personal information.

Information may be shared with TikTok only as necessary to provide the account connection and content-upload functions requested by the authorised user.

Information may also be disclosed when required by law, to protect security, or to investigate misuse of the service.

5. Data storage and retention

Future Radar is currently a private desktop tool. Information may be stored locally on the authorised user's computer.

Authorisation information is retained only while necessary to maintain the requested account connection. Temporary upload data is retained only as long as reasonably necessary to complete the requested operation or diagnose an error.

6. Security

Reasonable technical and organisational measures are used to protect information against unauthorised access, loss, alteration or disclosure.

No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

7. User choices and rights

Users may revoke Future Radar's access through their TikTok account settings or by discontinuing use of the service.

Depending on applicable law, users may have rights to request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal information.

8. Children's privacy

Future Radar is not intended for use by children. The service is intended only for authorised users who are able to manage the connected social-media account.

9. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the service or its data practices change. The effective date at the top of this page identifies the latest revision.

10. Contact

Privacy questions or requests may be submitted through the official Future Radar TikTok account: @TheFutureRadar.
